ABOUT

I decided to enter the world of programming in mid-2014 after I was challenged to try programming for 1 month. Within a week of the challenge, I fell in love. Since then, I have become a Chapter Leader of my local Girl Develop It, I've taught an Introduction to Programming workshop and will be teaching another in September. I have also TA'd for a short Javascript class. I've gone through Skillcrush's Ruby on Rails Developer Blueprint and I'm currently working through Free Code Camp's Full Stack certification program using Javascript.

I come from a long history of customer service positions. My passion has always been to help people and my goal is to combine that with my growing technical skills. I made the switch to technology because I crave challenges and mental stimulation.

In my free time, I love hanging out with my dogs, reading, and board games.
